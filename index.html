Mindful Eating

Welcome to Mindful Eating!

Mindful eating is NOT about

  • calculating calories
  • always eating healthy food
  • being perfect

Mindful eating means

  • paying attention to your food on purpose
  • focusing on your sensual awareness
  • feeling the experience of eating

It's easy to eat mindlessly

During a meal, a few questions to ask yourself

What's the texture?

What's the taste?

Do you notice any hunger or fullness body signals?

Try to practice these while you are having a meal











What can mindful eating bring you?

Let's review!

Do you often devour while watching TV shows?

Do you often eat while worrying about work?

Do you often eat on the road?

Have you used food to just satisfy emotional needs?

Mindless Eating Mindful Eating
Eating on autopilot or while multitasking Focusing all your attention on your food and the experience of eating
Eating to fill an emotional void Eating only to satisfy physical hunger
Eating junk or comfort food Eating nutritionally healthy meals and snacks
Eating food as quickly as possible Eating slowly, savoring every bite
Eating until all the food has gone, ignoring your body’s signals of fullness Listening to your body’s signals and eating only until you’re full
