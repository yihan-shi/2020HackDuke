ME - Mindful Eating

Paying attention to our food, on purpose,

moment by moment, without judgment is an approach to food

that focuses on individuals' sensual awareness of the food

and their experience of the food.

➀ Do you often devour a whole pizza while watching TV shows?

➁ Do you often stress eat while worrying about work?

➂ Do you often eat on the road?

➃ Have you ever reach to dessert just to satisfy emotional void?

What's the texture? / What's the taste? / Do you notice any hunger or fullness body signals? / What do various kinds of food bring you?

Mindless Eating Mindful Eating
Eating on autopilot or while multitasking Focusing all your attention on your food and the experience of eating
Eating to fill an emotional void Eating only to satisfy physical hunger
Eating junk or comfort food Eating nutritionally healthy meals and snacks
Eating food as quickly as possible Eating slowly, savoring every bite
Eating until all the food has gone, ignoring your body’s signals of fullness Listening to your body’s signals and eating only until you’re full
